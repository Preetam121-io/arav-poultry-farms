Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

For eggs, we accept orders starting from 10 trays (300 eggs). For live layers and broiler chicken, the minimum order is 50 kg. We offer special pricing for bulk orders above 500 eggs or 100 kg.

Do you deliver to my area?

We currently serve Chennai, Bangalore, and Hyderabad with same-day or next-day delivery depending on order timing. Contact us to confirm delivery availability in your specific locality.

How fresh are the eggs?

All eggs are collected daily from our farm and dispatched within 24 hours. We guarantee maximum freshness with proper grading and packaging to maintain quality during transit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash on delivery for verified customers. For regular bulk orders, we offer credit terms with advance payment options.

Are your birds and eggs certified?

Yes, our farm follows all bio-security protocols and hygiene standards. Our BV 300 breed is sourced from certified hatcheries, and we maintain complete health records for all our flocks.

Can I visit your farm?

We welcome farm visits by appointment for serious bulk buyers and partners. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to schedule a visit to our Karnataka facility.
